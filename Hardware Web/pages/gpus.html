Graphics Processing Unit: GPU

GPUs are dedicated pieces of hardware that are used to display graphics to your monitor.
They are also used to do complex mathematical equations. They are designed to do lots of maths quickly as that is what graphics is.
They are usually placed in the PCIe slots of the motherbpard.
